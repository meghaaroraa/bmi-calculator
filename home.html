Welcome to the BMI Calculator

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is a numerical value calculated based on a person's weight and height and is commonly used as an indicator of body fatness and potential health risks associated with weight. BMI is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters (BMI = weight in kg / (height in meters)^2). The resulting value is then used to categorize individuals into different BMI ranges, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obese, providing a general assessment of their weight status and potential health implications.
